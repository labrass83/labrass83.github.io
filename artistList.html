Biographies of
Famous Artists
Free

These short biographies of well known artists were created as a teaching tool. Many of the featured artists have been highlighted by the Meet The Masters art program. These biographies may be printed off for yourself or to enhance instruction. There is nothing to pay. Everything offered on this page is free. However, please do not sell for personal profit any of these items. They are offered for teaching or learning purposes only.
